Why Seattle Humane

We don't just shelter animals. We keep families together.

Founded in 1897, Seattle Humane has spent 127 years being a safety net for pets and the people who love them. We are more than an adoption center: we are a full-service resource for the entire region, with programs that meet families exactly where they are.

Your questions, answered honestly.

How is my monthly gift used?+
71 cents of every dollar we spend goes directly to the care of animals and community programming. Your monthly gift funds adoptions, food bank meals, vet care, and family support programs throughout the King County region.
Can I cancel anytime?+
Yes, absolutely. You can cancel, pause, or update your gift at any time by emailing donations@seattlehumane.org or calling (425) 373-5382. No questions asked, no hassle, ever.
Is my donation tax-deductible?+
Yes. Seattle Humane is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. Our EIN is 91-0282060. You will receive a donation receipt at the beginning of each year for your records.
What is the Furmily community?+
The Furmily is Seattle Humane's family of monthly donors. Members receive special quarterly updates, reduced fundraising mail, and behind-the-scenes stories about the animals their gifts are helping every month.
How do I update my payment information?+
You can log in to your online donor portal at seattlehumane.donorsupport.co to view and update your payment information anytime. You can also call (425) 373-5382 and our team will help you immediately.
How soon will I see the impact of my gift?+
Right away. Within 90 days of joining, you will receive your first quarterly impact update with stories of animals your gift helped. Furmily members at the Paw Partner level and above receive monthly behind-the-scenes updates.
What makes Seattle Humane different from other shelters?+
We are a full-service resource center, not just a shelter. We run a pet food bank distributing nearly 1 million meals annually, a low-cost veterinary care center, a temporary foster program, dog training, humane education, and financial assistance for families in need.
Can I volunteer in addition to giving monthly?+
We would love that! Seattle Humane welcomes volunteers for animal care, events, the pet food bank, foster care, and more. Visit seattlehumane.org/get-involved/volunteer to learn about current opportunities.
How does the Furmily program work financially for the organization?+
Monthly giving is the most cost-effective form of support we receive. It allows us to plan ahead, reduce operating uncertainty, and channel more resources directly into programs rather than emergency fundraising. Nearly 80% of our revenue comes from grassroots donations and planned gifts from people like you.
Does Seattle Humane accept surrendered pets?+
Yes. We also offer pet rehoming services and work hard to keep pets with their owners whenever possible through our financial assistance and community programs. Our goal is always to find the best outcome for the animal and the family.
